About Ras El Hanout

Ras El Hanout is a dynamic band hailing from the Middle East, blending the traditional sounds of Turkey with the groovy rhythms of jazz and fusion. The band is made up of five talented musicians who bring their unique styles and influences to the table, creating a fresh and original sound that is sure to get you moving on the dance floor.

Frontman Elad Cohen is a virtuoso on the clarinet, bringing his intricate melodies and passionate playing to the forefront of the band's sound. Bassist Bar Geva provides the driving force behind the music, layering in complex grooves and funky bass lines. Drummer Matan Deree adds a rhythmic pulse to the mix, using a variety of traditional Middle Eastern instruments to create a pulsing, energetic sound.

Joshua Levy (Keyboards and Synths) and Asher Farber (Baglama and Oud) round out the band, adding layers of intricate melodies and shimmering soundscapes to the mix. Together, the band has crafted a unique and captivating sound that is sure to get audiences everywhere moving and grooving.
